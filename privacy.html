Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

1. Data Collection

Set Home Go Home uses your device's GPS location to provide navigation services. This data is processed locally on your device.

2. Location Data

Your location data is used only while the app is active to calculate distance and direction to your set "Home" point. We do not store or transmit your location data to any external servers.

3. Offline Storage

The app may use local storage or Cache API to save map tiles and your "Home" coordinates on your device for offline use.

4. Third-Party Services

We use OpenStreetMap for map tiles and Overpass API for finding nearby places (POI). These services may receive your coordinates when you request map data or search for nearby places.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: master8383@example.com (Vervang dit door je eigen e-mail).
